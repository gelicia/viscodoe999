What are Colorado's 90/90/90 schools?

Loading Data...

Economic disadvantage can prevent some students from performing well in school. 90/90/90 are schools where students are succeeding, despite high poverty levels among their student bodies. The term 90/90/90 traditionally refers to the following three characteristics:

  • At least 90% of the school's population is on the Free Reduced Lunch program
  • At least 90% of the school's population is an ethnic minority
  • At least 90% of the school's population have achieved high academic standards

This tool allows you to see what schools fall into this category, and adjust the three requirements to see what schools qualify with different rules.

But, the last requirement has been changed. Instead of using the grades of the school's population, the last requirement draws from the school grade information supplied. The scale of the grade letters also uses the grading scale supplied.

Drag the slider to set the minimum requirements and see the schools that fit.
